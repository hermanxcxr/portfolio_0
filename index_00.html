Services

DATA MINING

When there is not enough information; may be required to capture data from other sources with python scripts, for these tasks, I use libraries such as SELENIUM, SCRAPY, REQUESTS, BS4 and languages such as XPATH

DATA ANALYSIS & ANALYTICS

These are the processes that allow us to detect patterns in the behavior of one or more variables, to give guidance on decisions that should be taken in the future. Some procedures are sampling tools, hypothesis testing, clustering, principal component analysis, etc. But also we can create prediction models based on data, like regressions, decision trees, neural networks, among others.

DATA VIZ

Show the conclusions obtained through the data in an understandable way for the audience and if necessary also interactively; the tools to use for technical topics are matplotlib, seaborn; but also can be used Tableau, DataStudio and PowerBI.

About me

Hi! I'm Herman, electronic engineer and master of business administration.

My experience managing projects, taught me the importance of transforming data into information, to improve the performance in the company processes and operations.

This inspired me to dedicate myself fully to data analysis, giving me the opportunity to help companies in their success and to guide them into a data driven philosophy.
